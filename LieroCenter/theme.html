Here's my Campaign Archive(Theme packs):

These are "Theme Packs", which one way or another, alter more than one aspect of the Liero game to create a whole new theme. These are more fun to try out but usually more complicated to get running.
Because many Theme Packs are different, each description will let you know how the packages come and what will be required (if anything).
To open these files, you need Winzip.
