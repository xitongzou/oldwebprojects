Tenkaichi Budoukai - The Rules

Of course, there is always a catch. You have to go by some rules and regulations to make this a fair fight. You are disqualified if you have a weapon or use any kind of object to help you win. If you leave the tournament and if you kill your opponent, you lose. You have lost when you have been knocked unconscious, or, go down from the count of 10. Also, you have lost when, you get knocked out of the ring or say, "I give up/Mercy" Oh, and uh, you can't do this: poke your opponent in the eyes, or, hit anyone in the family jewels. When - The 21st through the 23rd Tenkaichi Budoukais take place in the Dragon Ball saga. The 24th through the 28th Tenkaichi Budoukai take place in Dragon Ball Z. They are held on May 7 every three years.
