Dragonball Z Final Battle

Battle of Ma Junior/Piccolo


Fighters:
Good: Son Goku and Kami
Bad: Ma Junior/Piccolo

(During this fight, I will refer to Ma Junior as Piccolo)

Act 1: Ma Junior Comes to Attack on Son Goku
This act is basically, a "warm-up." Goku and Piccolo exchange blows left and right, never really firing any ki at each other. After Piccolo has had enough of Goku's "playing" he decides to increases the odds a little.

Act 2: Piccolo Eats Kami and Son Goku Tricks Him
The real fight begins. Goku and Piccolo are hard at work trying to beat each other. Piccolo wouldn't care if he lost the tournament in exchange for Goku's life, but Goku wants to beat him, win the tournament, and then stop Piccolo from taking over the world. Just as it seems hopeless, Kami steps in, and, defends Goku, telling him, he will do the Mafuuba. After successfully doing it, Piccolo begins to go into the denshi jar, but Kami doesn't realize how strong Piccolo is, and, is counter-attacked to be put into the denshi jar. Piccolo then eats the jar, causing Kami to never come back. After a short fight, Piccolo decides to grow to an enormous size and force Goku out of the ring. Goku has an idea, and it works perfectly. He jumps up into Piccolo's mouth and gets back the denshi jar. Kami is saved.

Act 3: Piccolo Blasts the Arena and Goku Away
A very short act. This act just shows how strong Piccolo is, by destroying the ring and causing Goku to be hurt and scared a bit. After the ring is destroyed, Goku then decides to try and cripple Piccolo. He, instead, breaks off Piccolos arm, thinking he has got him armless. Piccolo then shows off his regeneration technique. Goku realizes that he cannot win by that then. This excites Goku a bit.

Act 4: Piccolo and Son Goku Fight Each Other to the Death
This act is very bloody. Piccolo legs and face show lots of blood, as Goku is firing him at all he has. But, Piccolo does just the same, and makes a big, gaping hole in Goku's chest. It just continues like that from there, causing them both extreme pains.

Act 5: Son Goku Retaliates and Defeats Piccolo
Piccolo gives it his all here, and, decides he doesn't care. He will decide to blow up everyone and everything around him, even if it kills him. After gathering the necessary ki, Piccolo then fires a blast so big, that Goku almost didn't make it. But, Goku even surprised himself and everyone else here. He learned how to fly. He flew straight up, without jumping or ki and hit Piccolo so hard; it made him fly back, out of the ring. Piccolo was stunned and so was Goku. After receiving a seznu, Goku was on his feet and feeling great. Goku had won his first Tenkaichi Budoukai fight. Piccolo, on the other hand, was crippled, paralyzed, and very sore. Goku felt sorry for him, and gave him a senzu. Piccolo told him he would regret it and flew off.
