Dragonball Z Final Battle

Battle of Piccolo Daimou

Fighters:
Good: Son Goku and Tenshinhan
Bad: Dorumu and Piccolo Daimou

Act 1: Tenshinhan Fights Until Goku Comes to the Rescue
Tenshinhan tries in all his efforts to try and kill Piccolo Daimou, but has no luck. Dorumu is really beating him up, to the point of death. Just when Dorumu is about to deliver the final blow, Goku shows up. After training at Korin Tower, Goku has some new tricks to show them all. After zanzoukening out of sight, he reappears in front of Dorumu, and, knocks him out. This knocking out also has the loss of Dorumu's eye. In other words, Goku kicks him so hard, his left eye flies out. This makes Piccolo Daimou very pissed.

Act. 2: Piccolo Daimou Attacks Son Goku
This is just a mini-fight between Goku and Piccolo, and nothing really big happens. Nothing, except for that Goku is beating Piccolo up badly. Goku even attempts to throw Piccolo long distances and deflecting ki blasts to go back at Piccolo.

Act 3: Piccolo Attacks Son Goku and Tenshinhan Comes to Save the Day
Now, the battle gets intense. Piccolo tries all of his best moves on Goku, and, sometimes he does actually hit Goku. One time was, when Goku couldn't see Piccolo's blast from behind, and, Tenshinhan had to step in. Tien saved Goku's life, and, made Piccolo even more upset. Now, it was all or nothing. Piccolo was going to start to do some serious evil works.

Act 4: Piccolo Daimou's Trump Card Against Son Goku
Piccolo fires a blast straight at Tenshinhan, causing him to be paralyzed. Goku still cannot move real well, but won't give up. Piccolo then grabs Tien's head, and, starts to make demands. Piccolo will let Tien go, only if Goku gives up his own life. If he doesn't, Tien's head will be crushed without hesitation. Goku, being noble as he is, says he won't give up. Piccolo demands he thinks it over again, and Goku says he has already made up his mind. Piccolo decides to cripple Goku as well. He shoots a ki blast straight into Goku's knee, causing Goku to not being able to move. Goku doesn't have any choice, but, to give up now.

Act 5: Son Goku Finally Kills Piccolo Daimou
Piccolo asks what does Goku want to do, and, Goku tells him that he will give up. Piccolo agrees with him, and puts Tien down. Piccolo starts his flight into the air, and, starts to build up enormous ki. Goku remembers all the people that he hurt and will hurt if he doesn't do something. His only choice is to shoot a Kamehameha at him, but his left arm is broken. So, he starts to power up a Kamehameha in his feet. After gathering the necessary energy, Goku lets loose the Kamehameha, and starts to fly at Piccolo. Piccolo cannot believe what he is seeing and lets the ki energy he has now at Goku. Goku, however, didn't give up, and, flew higher and higher until he connected with Piccolo, and, went straight through Piccolo, leaving a big hole in Piccolo. This was a mortal wound and was making Piccolo loose allot of power, even dieing. Piccolo could only spit up an egg that would soon be our next enemy, in our next final battle.
