Dragonball Z Final Battle

Battle of Radditz


Fighters:
Good: Son Goku, Son Gohan, and Piccolo
Bad: Radditz

Act 1: Son Goku's Child is Kidnapped and Piccolo Comes to Help
Son Goku thought he lived a normal life, as an Earthling. But, he soon was to be reminded, that he was an alien that had a brother. Goku didn't want to accept it, so, his brother threatened him with his only son. After Radditz stole his brother's son, he left and went to his space ship. Piccolo had already encountered Radditz, and, knew that Goku would need help. However, he told Goku that he didn't want to help him; he just wanted to help Goku so this man could be gone and he could try and destroy the world again. They fly off, hoping that both of their goals will be met.

Act 2: The Saiyan Fights the Good Guys
Piccolo and Goku are on the battlefield. Radditz has total confidence he will win. The hero's son is crying and locked away in a space pod. This can only be DBZ. The exchanges of blows are made, with Radditz being in the favor. When it seems all lost, Piccolo asks Goku if he has a secret move. Goku says he has been settling down and hasn't trained as much. Piccolo calls him soft and wants to do the attack that he designed for Goku's execution to his own brother.

Act 3: Piccolo Fires His Makkankosappo
Piccolo asks Goku to go and fight his brother, while Piccolo powers up for his ultimate attack. After both brothers have fought enough for Goku to fall to the ground, Piccolo is ready to attack. Goku tells him to go, so, Piccolo does. After about 3 seconds, the attack is done, and, smoke is everywhere. Piccolo looks to see the Radditz is still standing, after performing a very strong attack. Radditz tells him it was all in vain and you will lose.

Act 4: Son Goku Takes a Beating but Tricks His Brother
Goku is on the ground, being kicked to death. Piccolo is just watching in horror, knowing that if he tries his attack again, he will dodge. Gohan is getting seriously pissed off, and, cannot take it anymore. Gohan breaks through his pod, and, goes straight for his uncle. After giving a very hard blow to the stomach, Radditz cannot move for a second. He then regains control, and, starts his attack on his nephew. Gohan gets kicked for about 20-30 feet away, causing Goku now to be angry. Radditz walks toward Gohan going to give the death blow, when, Goku gets up and grab his brother. Goku say he is stupid for letting his guard down.

Act 5: Son Goku Makes the Ultimate Sacrifice to Save the Earth
Goku demands that Piccolo does that attack again, and, Piccolo starts before Goku could even tell him. Radditz then tries to give every excuse to not hold on to him. Goku closes his ears to him, and, Piccolo is done with powering up his attack. Radditz tells him to not do it; he will end his life, and never come back. Goku shuns him away, and, is ready to die. Piccolo begins to yell Makkankosappo, and, fires the screw beam. Radditz and Goku are both hit, and, it goes through them both. It ends very bloody, and, after talking a bit about how Goku will come back, Piccolo kills him with the final blow.
