DragonBall GT TV Specials

DBGT TV Special: Goku's Supplement! Proof of Courage is the Four-Star Dragon Ball
Original Release Date: March 26,1997
Rating: 6 of 7

Story
A hundred years have passed since the end of DragonBall GT and the only character still alive from the series is Pan. Her grandson, Goku Jr., sets out to find the four-star DragonBall to save his ill grandmother by asking Shenron to save her. Unfortunately, Goku Jr. is very cowardly and unaware of his true power, unlike his great grandfather Goku. While on his journey, Goku Jr. is joined by Pahku, a bully who picks on him at school. Together they set out for Mt. Paozu where the four-star ball resides.

Thoughts
This movie has some great humor and the story is well done, despite the lack of familiar characters from the three DragonBall series. There's also deep emotion when Goku Jr. encounters his Great Grandfather.
