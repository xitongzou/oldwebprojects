Bardock


  1. Bardock is the father of Goku and Radditz. He worked for Freeza conquering planets for him with his group of saiyan warriors. Bardock is a little depressed after they send Radditz away and doesn't think that it'll do him any good if he develops a bond with Goku. But in the end he goes and see's Goku. Bardock is a scientist and is the inventor of the artificial moon that some of the saiyans use in the beggining of DBZ. Bardock was given the power to see into the future in order to see his own doom, by a long since diminished race. Bardock looks into the future and is shown that Freeza will destroy the planet Vegeta. So he gathers together a group of warriors and plans to stop Freeza. Freeza who is pissed off from recently being attacked by King Vegeta and his men, decides to go out and have a little fun. He throws a huge blast at the planet and the warriors infront of it automatically die. Before he dies Bardock see's a vision of his son. Knowing that he will be the one to beat Freeza he dies happy.

    2. Chi Chi


  2. Chi Chi is a human woman who is the wife of Goku and plays a minor role in Dragonball Z as the strict and protective mother and wife. She often picks fights with other people and is very strict about Gohans studies. She also has a second son named Goten and gets herself killed in the Buu Saga after running at Majin Buu. But she is later wished back by the Namek dragonballs.

He also makes a minor appearence in GT.

    8. Pikkon


  8. Pikkon trains under West Kai for many centuries gaining great strength, and becoming his greatest student. After the Cell Game, Goku goes off to find the Grand Kai to be trained by him. But when Goku finds that he has to train for 10,000 years before he can train under the Grand Kai, he knows he can't wait that long. But because of a little scermish between the Kai's which brings about a tournament of all the Kai's students. Goku participates, along with Pikkon and many other warriors who've earned the right to keep their bodies after death. Pikkon&Goku both made it to the finals and face off against each other. They both seem to have equal power, and Goku has to transform into his super saiyan mode after Paikuhan takes off his weighted clothes. The fight is in favor of Pikkon who is using his Thunder Ring against Goku. But after he does it a third time Goku finds a weakness, and fires a Kamehameha to land him outside the ring. But because they both touched the ceiling during the match, they are both disqualified. Pikkon also makes an appearence in movie #12.

    9. Tapion


  9. Tapion is a warrior from DBZ movie #13. He holds the evil monster Hildegarn within his body for many centuries, until Hoi an evil sorceror tricks the Z Warriors into using Shenron to open the magical box that holds Tapion. Tapion undergoes incredible pain as each time half of Hildegarn escapes, and he uses his magical ocarina to trap him once again. But since Tapions brother is dead, and he holds the other half Tapion cannot keep both seperate. He asks Trunks to finish him off before the two halfs join, but his plan backfires, and it's up to the Z Warriors to take on Hildegarn. But he proves too much for even Goku, and just when everything seems impossible Goku does a new technique and destroys Hildegarn. Tapion gives his sword to Trunks and says goodbye.
