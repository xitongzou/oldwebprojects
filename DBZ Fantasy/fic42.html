Chapter 1 - Recruitment

This fan fic is, like so many others, set in an alternate universe. This universe consists of the same planets, star systems, etc, but the inhabitants of the galaxy vary slightly from the original DBZ series. Vegeta and Goku are both saiyans, and Gohan is half saiyan. Both have surpassed every other person in their race by becoming super saiyans. The planet Vegeta  still remains intact though, as Frieza hasn’t seen the need to destroy it yet. The planet Vegeta isn’t called that though, it has had its name changed to Planet Saiya.

Frieza has the two most powerful henchmen in the world, and an elite squad of fighters. The power levels during this fan fic are not the same to the series, and this will be stressed time and time again. For instance – Zarbon could be stronger than Frieza in this fic, and Chao-zu could be even mightier than them both (Don’t worry, that’s not the case). Anyway, I’ve bored you enough with this epilogue, enjoy the rest of the fic!

************

The inhabitants of the Planet Saiya are content with their life. They wander around, getting along with their business under the watchful eye of Frieza’s guards. They work for him out of fear more than anything else. The conditions that they are put in are absurd, as they are forced to live on the dark side of the Planet, with Frieza’s soldiers occupying the light side.

The difference between these two sides are many. The light side is better for growing crops, living in, and well, basically everything. The dark side is terminally cold, dark, damp and dingy. There is very little food, and the quaint amount that can be scavenged is shared sparingly amongst the residents. 

The Planet Saiya wasn’t always like this. It once was a peaceful planet, where there was only a light side. Everything thrived and grew well. Then Frieza came along. He sucked the resources out of the planet, and caused this abnormality, where one side is in much better condition than the other. He laughed as the saiyans cowered in fear at him, and laughed even more when the bravest of the warriors had attacked him, as each one of them fell to his hand.

The saiyans have had enough of being treated like this, and they are all gathered inside a hut, of about the approximate size of Frieza’s ship. This is where our story begins…..

 

Three saiyans sit at a desk, one is an old man, with graying hair, another is a slightly younger one, but a lot smaller too, probably below average height, with long flowing hair down to his ankles. The third saiyan is a huge, muscular man, bigger than anyone else. He seems to intimidate the weaker of the species, but earns respect from the braver ones. On his arm are scratches with tally marks on, and initials all over. These are the names and numbers of all the people that he has killed in his lifetime. He is the most violent saiyan ever, and seems to be the leader of this pack. His name is Otoku. He slams his fist down on the table, snapping it in half. The benevolent chatting comes to a halt, and all eyes gaze upwards at the towering figure.

“This meeting was called to discuss what we do to Frieza…” Otoku started. “We must stop him now before he gets out of hand. We have already been wiped from the good lands onto the wastelands of our own planet, what is to stop him from wiping us off it altogether? I say that there is only one course of action, and that is war!” He kicks the table away, and raises his arm into the air. “Who will fight by my side, who will take on Frieza’s men, who will free our people, who will be the savior of our planet!?”. Cheers spread across the whole room, as volunteers start to come along thick and fast. A group of about 30 saiyans join Otoku, and the remaining saiyans (Mainly women and feeble lower class soliders) salute them, and cheer with all their heart. Otoku leads the brave volunteers out into the open air, and takes them into a small barracks. He stops outside it though, and waits for silence. When he is granted it, and begins to speak once more in that voice which is made for speeches.

“You are the bravest warriors on the planet. You are the ones who will take on Frieza by my side! You must work hard, as this will be no easy ride! If anyone can’t handle it, then drop out now, because any failures will be killed outright! Do I make myself clear?” Mumbles scatter around the crowd of the now-no-so enthusiastic soldiers as they attempt to answer, and overcome their fear. A couple of members from the group slowly back away from the crowd, trying to keep a low profile. The group loses only a small portion of its numbers, not significant enough to put any sort of ruptue in their plans. Otoku is still waiting for an answer, and is about to shout again when a young, male saiyan steps forward. He has long spiked hair that sticks up, and it is no shorter than a foot high. It sways calmly in the wind. He has a confident smirk on his face, and a scar running from his brow, down to his eye, and it seems to go right through it, as if it cuts through the actual pupil, and then down his cheek, until it is about level with his lips.

“Are we going to get this done?” the young saiyan asks. “I am Korosu, and Frieza has killed my whole family! I have seen how powerful he is, and I know for a fact that no one, not even you Otoku, is strong enough to beat this monster. After he killed my father, I charged at him, but couldn’t connect with a single blow.” His voice is becoming sentimental now, and he is obviously trying to hold back the tears as he continues. “He backhanded me away, swatted me away, just like a fly. I was no match for him, and I know that your power level may be a few thousand more than mine Otoku, but you are still no match for him. If we are to defeat this evil monster, then we must train day and night, with all we have. We must train until we have power levels of over 100,000. Only then can we come close to matching his power.”

The other saiyans look at him in despair. Most of them only have a power level of 3,000. Korosu himself has a power level of 10,000, as he is well known to be one of the best fighters around. Otaku’s true strength has never been revealed, but legend has it that he has defeated some of the strongest soldiers in the galaxy. Of course, this is just a legend, and may not be true.

Everyone cheers, as they head off into the barracks to start their training. Otaoku stops Korosu though, and whispers into his ear.

“Kid, don’t get these guys’ hopes up. I know the fight will be futile, but I will fight until death, and if we put our all into it, we may just defeat him. You’re right though, even I am a long way off the power required to so much as match Frieza. Now come on, we have to train these guys, they’re a sight for soar eyes!” The two of them sprint into the building, and incessant talking can be heard from the outside, as they discuss training tactics.

***

Zarbon, an upper class soldier, is talking to one of his fellow elites. This elite isn’t anyone too special, but he is there to help in case any minor trouble breaks out. Zarbon seems to get slightly annoyed at the constant rambling that this soldier is emitting, so he holds out his hand, and kills the elite in one shot. Zarbon then dusts his hands, and starts walking towards Frieza’s quarters to accompany his overlord. He is intercepted on the way though, by an eager little boy, only just into his teenage years. The boy has a hearty smile on his face, and he looks up at Zarbon with a great degree of respect in his eyes.

“Master Zarbon” the boy started. “I have just got news that the saiyans are preparing for rebellion. A group of them have gathered in a barracks on their planet. Word has come from the Planet Saiya that their first target will be either you or Frieza.”

Zarbon smiles at this news, and the smile turns into laughter. The young boy looks on with concern, wondering how his master isn’t in the slightest bit worried. Zarbon finishes his spell of laughter, then turns to the boy, and puts his hand on his shoulder. The little boys concern turns to joy, as his greatest hero has touched him.

“Tenoki, you have been working in my service for a few years now, and you have been a good servant. I thank you for the information that you have gathered, but I must question you on one thing – From what sources did you scavenge it from?”

The boy looks slightly taken back by this comment. “Zarbon, I have never lied to you in all the years that I have been in your employment. It is against my better judgment to do anything dishonest and wrong. I have been informed by one of the soldiers monitoring the planet. I scavenged it off no one, but was told it by a high ranked guard.” Zarbon is looking puzzled by this last statement.

“A guard told you this vital information?”

“Sort of”

“What do you mean ‘Sort of’?”

“I overheard him raving about it. He said something like….‘Blasted saiyans! It looks as though they’re gathering for a rebellion!’ then I ran straight to find you.”

“Ok, go prepare my battle uniform Tenoki, I may be paying our saiyan friends a little visit.” The boy nods, and rushes off in the opposite direction to where Zarbon is heading. Zarbon quickens his walking pace, as he knows that this information could be extremely important. His cocky stride makes his braid of green hair bounce up and down with each step, as his light green skin shines in the obscene amount of light that is given off from the powerful lamps on the ships walls. Zarbon walks round a corner, and then enters a large room. He takes a deep breath, then starts to blurt out the news.

After Zarbon has finished talking, a chair swivels round, letting off a small squeak, so that the occupant of it is now facing him. Frieza, the evil tyrant gets up out of the chair, and walks towards the see-through glass of his living quarters, brushing past his loyal henchman. He gazes outside whilst holding a small glass of red wine in his hand, and delicately sipping it occasionally. He turns to his minion, Zarbon, and notices that he is shaking.

“What is wrong Zarbon? You don’t seem to be your usual self.”

“It’s this saiyan rebellion. We all know how powerful they have the potential to become, and they may have the power to take me out if I meet a big gang of them”

“Impossible” Frieza insisted. “After all, at the moment, it is only a rumour. I want you to go and check it out. Take with you the Ginyu Force. Between you, you are the most elite fighters in the world. There is no one who can defeat you, so don’t worry. Worry causes failure, and you had better not fail me Zarbon!”

“N…no sir. I shall perform the mission to perfection.” Something about his voice sounded edgy. Zarbon has beads of sweat dripping down his face. His fists are clenched hard, and he nods his head, then turns for the door. “I shall prepare for the mission now master Frieza.”

“Good, I will send you an alert tomorrow, before you leave for the Planet Saiya. Be prepared though Zarbon, I don’t want to lose a good soldier to a band of unruly monkeys.” Zarbon fakes a small chuckle at this comment, as he walks out of the door.

Zarbon is furious at Frieza. How dare he push him around like that, and threaten him in such a way. Who is he to demand success? What if there are difficulties? What if he can’t defeat the saiyans? So what if Frieza regards this saiyans as mere monkeys. One of them may even grow strong enough to defeat him, and knock him off his throne, halting his reighn of terror. That is a long way off though, and until then, there is no point in resisting, or disobeying orders, as Frieza is a much tougher prospect that any saiyan could ever be.

Zarbon power walks down the corridor, thinking of the mission all the way to his quarters. He enters them, and is ready to get a good night’s sleep until he is forced to spy on the ‘Unruly monkeys’. He closes the door, and doesn’t even bother to change, as he flops onto his soft, comfortable bed, with his head filled with constant thinking, and images of Frieza’s wrath. The last person to disobey him was tortured in a terrible way, then killed mercilessly. That is not the sort of thing that he wants to happen to him. Zarbon turns over, then rests his eyes, making a resolution to train harder than ever after the mission, in order to become stronger, and to finish his mentor – Frieza. That day shall be grand, but first these saiyans must be dealt with.

Chapter 2 - Arrival

A small, rounded pod comes soaring towards the Planet Saiya, turning into a raging ball of flames as it breaks into the atmosphere. It docks down in an isolated clearing, with huge impact. A crater about the size of an Oozaru is made. After a short pause, there is movement from the pod. The door swings open, and a light green hand appears on the side. It thrusts the attachment of its body up, and then finishes climbing out of the pod with a small leap. The passenger looks around, breathing in the dirty air from the surface of the industrial side of the planet. The man is none other than Zarbon. He walks slowly out of the crater as 5 pods crash down behind him. He doesn’t even hesitate to look round, and sees that they are on his team.

‘Damn, I’m jumpy today! Gotta calm myself down!’ Zarbon thought to himself as he finished getting out of the crater that his crash landing had made. He bends over, and kneels down. He runs his smooth skin through the dusty ground, and his investigation achieves only a clump of eroded dirt. He sighs to himself as he gets to his feet, and casually wonders towards the Ginyu Force.

The Ginyu Forces pods are unlike any that Zarbon had ever seen. They had streaks of blue down the side, and had the ever-prominent logo engraved onto the side. Each one seemed to be tailor-made for each member, with decorations of all sorts on. Almost systematically, each one of the pod doors swing open. One-by-one, the Force get out, and line up. Zarbon knows what’s coming, and gives off a grievous sigh.

‘Everytime they go anywhere, these idiots have to do all that posing. Why can’t they just get along with the missions!’ Zarbon contemplated. ‘Must be because of some sort of mental deficiency’ he confirmed to himself.

The elite soldiers of the Ginyu Force started to dance around in that annoying pose that they were guaranteed to do wherever they were. After a tedious performance, Ginyu proudly leads the troops towards Zarbon. With an arrogant grin on his face, Ginyu does a mocking bow. Zarbon is not impressed, and kicks the captain in the face, with utmost hatred. Ginyu staggers back, but knows not to fight back, as it would be worthless. Zarbon is very close to Frieza, and if they were to agitate him, then they could be in big trouble with the head honcho.

Zarbon turns, flicking his braid of hair into the air, then walks off, with each stride more ignorant than the last. He has beads of sweat dripping down his face, and his fists are clenched hard.

‘These Ginyu idiots will under estimate those blasted saiyan’ Zarbon interpreted. ‘They will be destroyed! Ah well, that’s good for me, at least I won’t have to go on any more missions with them’ The changeling smirked at the prospect of finally ridding himself of these posing goofs.

They all set off towards their destination - the saiyan camp, with the whole of the Ginyu Force lagging behind slightly, so as to not anger Zarbon anymore. He sluggishly kicks the ground, and starts to psyche himself up mentally, so that he is in the best possible state for what will probably be a huge brawl.

In the head barracks, Otaku and Korosu are hard at work training with their maximum effort. Sweat pours down both mans face, as they hang from the ceiling, doing sit ups. Korosu mentally counts his 10,000th sit up, and assumes that he has done enough of them for now. He is suspended in the air by the rope attached to his feet. He breathes hard, trying to regain his breath so that he can get back to training. Otaku comes up to him to help him down, and cuts the rope which he hangs from with a handy pocket knife. Korosu falls to the floor, and thanks his elder warrior sarcastically. Saiyans litter the area, with their hard working nature, as they become more and more powerful.

“Hey kid, want to test out that new found strength that you have attained in the past few days?” Otaku asked.

“Yeah, ok, me vs. you. I’ll kick you round the floor my friend” taunted Korosu.

“Deal then. Everyone, stop training immediately!” Shouted Otaku. “Me and Korosu are about to show you lot how to fight. One of us is going to be beaten to a pulp, and put in the regeneration tank for about half of a day.”

“What?!”

“Ha, don’t worry kid, what doesn’t kill you, only makes you stronger! Now let’s begin!”

Otaku charges at Korosu, and knees him in the stomach before the rookie gets a chance to say anything. Korosu falls to the ground, holding his stomach, desperately trying to catch a breath. He begins to cough up blood, and looks down to the injury. His armour has been broken off very cleanly. He looks up to Otaku pleadingly, and the leader of the rebel forces kicks Korosu in the face. He falls back holding his nose as blood gushes from it. His face is a bloody mess, and he looks up at his sensei with a look of discretion in his eyes. He tries to talk to Otaku, but is unable because of the blood that is spilling from his mouth. Otaku grabs Korosu, and slaps him round the face, causing more bleeding. Korosu is hanging limply now, as his saiyan master holds him high in the air for all to see.

“This is an example of an unprepared being. Do you really think that Frieza will wait until you are ready before he strikes? He will show no mercy, and tear each of you limb from limb! Look at this weakling here! He wasn’t able to put his heart into it, because of his emotions. I don’t want any of you to feel these emotions, as they will only make you weak! Now, the next training exercise is…….” Otaku’s voice trails off as he lets off a loud gasp. Korosu’s fist is in his gut. His eyes widen, but his look of excruciating pain soon turns into a sly grin, as he grabs the arm of his fellow saiyan elite, and chops it with such force that the crack could probably be heard from miles away. The scream of pain that followed it was equally as loud, piercing the souls of all the frightened saiyans. Korosu falls to the floor, holding his arm whilst breathing extremely fast. He doesn’t seem to be able to get up now, and Otaku holds out his hand, and starts to build up energy in it. Korosu mouths the words ‘Please, stop’ to his master, but they are not received. A blast of blue energy goes straight through the saiyan soldiers chest, and makes even more blood scatter everywhere, plastering the walls, and making everyone wince at the sight. Otaku shouts over the disgruntled mumbles so that his voice can be heard.

“Get him to the regeneration tank….NOW!” He then bends down to Korosu’s level, and whispers into his ear. “This will make you stronger, maybe even more so than me” He looks up to the cowering saiyans, whom he considered to be scum, and shouts at them even louder than before. “Hurry up and get this brave warrior to a medical tank! You people make me sick! Now train harder!” 

The saiyans re-gather their nerve, and efficiently lift Korosu up, and take him to the medical lab. He is put into a tank, and left in the hands of doctors. Otaku walks up to one of them and gestures him over to the corner, away from the crowd.

“He will be ok, right? I mean, he’d better not die, because if he does…”

“It’s too early to tell. He looks pretty badly hurt, and if he was taken here a minute later, then I would guarantee death, but as it is, it is impossible to tell. I will keep you posted on anything that happen here, no matter how trivial”

“Thank you doctor” Otaku said as he left for the door. He called for his saiyan army, and they all jogged back to the barracks, to continue their training, unaware of the evil that has landed on their planet, and is rapidly approaching to uncover the conspiracy plot, and to destroy those wretched saiyans once and for all!

Chapter 3 - Mass Destruction

Zarbon led the Ginyu Force into a somewhat isolated village. People are going about their daily business here, buying groceries, chatting about local events, and running around, playing abruptedly. This side of the planet is extremely dark, and there is very little enjoyment in the smoggy, thick air. Zarbon trembles a little, not from the terrible cold, but out of fear. They venture forth into the unknown abyss, noticing something very strange about the inhabitants of this place. They all have brown, furry tails, which swish up and down, and blow in the wind.

“This is the place,” Zarbon said. “Look out for any sort of military building” The Ginyu Force nodded, and they began to scout the place, desperately hunting for their target so they can get this relatively boring mission over and done with.

It takes a while, but Zarbon eventually hears grunts from a large building, and decides to take a peek inside. He notices all the activity going on, and gestures over towards the Ginyu Force with his hand. They silently approach him, and wait patiently behind him. Zarbon sees that the activity is that of hard-working scum - The saiyans, who are training with great amounts of effort. He bursts through the door, and grabs the nearest resident of the barracks.

“What the hell are you doing here?” he yelled at the top of his voice. The saiyan cowers in fear though, scared of what is to come. Zarbon asks again, but it warrants no response. He throws the saiyan through the eastern wall of the barracks, and then turns to the watching eyes of the other occupants of this building. They all stop what they are doing, and get into their fighting stances. Zarbon laughs at them, and waves his arm towards the outside. In walks Captain Ginyu, bleeding nose and all, along with the rest of his elite fighting squad. “I assume you are the ones that Frieza told me to check on,” laughed Zarbon.

“Who are you?” asked a strong sounding voice. There is no answer, so the voice asks again, this time in a much harsher way.  “Who are you?” The sound is coming from some shadows to the left of the room. The figure is only revealing his silhouette, but from it, Zarbon can see that this warrior is evidently very strong. The warrior walks out into the light letting it illuminate his face. “I am Otaku. Leave now, or we shall be forced to eject you from the premises by way of physical violence.”

“You aren’t scaring anyone, scum. I am Zarbon, elite soldier, and henchman of Frieza. These acquaintances of mine are the Ginyu Force, more elite soldiers, each with a power level of four times what yours is. If you wish to do battle, then we shall. Now step down, and come with us quietly.”

“Fuck you” Otaku replied.

“Have it your way,” muttered Zarbon, as he charged in, and buried his fist into Otaku’s gut. The huge saiyan goes down to one knee, but doesn’t give up. Otaku grabs the arm of Zarbon, and begins to crush it, making some blue blood trickle from the wound caused. Screaming out in pain, Zarbon blasted Otaku in the face with a small ball of energy. Otaku immediately falls backwards, in a crumpled heap.

“Anyone else wish to oppose us?” Zarbon asked cockily. “I thought not. Now everyone line up….DO IT!” The saiyans all stand in a line, waiting to be led outside. There is a chain message going along their ranks, as if they are planning something.

“NOW!” a saiyan elite shouted. All together, they blasted their most powerful beam at the Ginyu Force. Zarbon looks on as the whole of the force are obliterated. It takes a while for the dust to clear, but when it does, only Captain Ginyu emerges from the piles of rubble. He is covered in his own purple blood, and is having major trouble standing up. The rest of his men aren’t moving. Jeice, Rakoome, Burta, Guldo, not even one of them are showing any signs of life.

“You bastards! I’m not going to let you get away with this!” Ginyu yelled as he held his arms above his head. A ball of florescent purple energy is gathering in the air, and when it reaches its maximum size (About the height of a bookcase) he throws it, maintaining style by posing as the ball leaves his hands. The saiyans can only look on in horror as the ball speeds towards them, and makes contact, causing mass damage, and an epic explosion.

“You idiot Ginyu!” shout Zarbon. “Why’d you do that?”

“We were going to kill them anyway,” replied the captain quietly.

“Yes, but not here! We were meant to kill them outside to prevent anymore thought of rebellion!” shouted Zarbon, somewhat unconvincingly.

“Don’t worry, everyone would have seen that blast”

“I’m not surprised, you blew the roof off, quite literally”

“Yeah, it’s not easy being a super soldier” Ginyu chuckled. Zarbon couldn’t believe how the saiyans managed to kill four members of his squad. Four of the toughest warriors in the galaxy destroyed by some mere monkeys.

“Ginyu, listen carefully, we must make sure that there are no survivors, because if there are then there is no way that Frieza will let us live!”

“I know. I hope one of these assholes still is alive though, then I can torture him for what he and his friends have done to my team!” Ginyu shouted, with clenched fists as he wondered around the room, checking pulses. There seemed to be no one left alive. Ginyu walked towards the final saiyan, the troublemaker at the start of the fight, and checks his pulse. It is beating, beating slowly, but it is beating none-the-less. Ginyu lifts Otaku up, and starts to choke him against the wall. Zarbon notices this out of the corner of his eye, and sighs. ‘Why does this idiot have to pick on the helpless ones? Why can’t he just finish up so we can go back’ he thought to himself. Otaku half opens one eye, and lets out a blast from the palm of his hand, piercing the armour of captain Ginyu, and knocking him down onto the floor. Before he gets a chance to take a breath, Otaku has a hole blown through his stomach by a KI blast of awesome power. Zarbon stands with his palm held out, with smoke drifting skywards from it.

“Damn saiyan. Ginyu had better not be dead!” Zarbon thought out loud. He runs over to the Captain, and raises his head.

“Zar….Zarbon…..help me quickly…” Ginyu pleaded helplessly. Zarbon lifts him up, puts him on his shoulder, and shoots away through the air, dumping Ginyu in the small, rounded pod upon his arrival on the landing site. Zarbon keys in some controls instantaneously, then slams the door shut. Ginyu’s pod takes off with remarkable speed, heading towards the docking ports of Frieza’s ship.

“I just hope that I have done enough. These blasted saiyan have better not have taken away another one of our best men. I may not like him, but he is a great fighter, almost as good as me in fact…..why did he have to try to torture the last one though, the damned idiot!” Zarbon said to himself, as he stepped into his own pod. He closed the door, then started to run his finger across the back of the seat, searching for the safety belt. He discovers that it has been caught underneath the seat, and with a huge yank, he pulls it out, and straps himself in. Zarbon taps in the co-ordinates to which he is heading (Frieza’s ship) then sits back, resting, as the pod takes off. He is going to have to explain what went wrong to Frieza, and it isn’t going to be pretty.

“Damn, why did Ginyu have to kill those saiyans?” Zarbon asked himself. “I was going to side with them against Frieza, but now I have no army, and must wait even longer! I must admit that they had astounding power in destroying the Ginyu Force, and the one who gave the captain such mortal wounds appeared to be very strong too. I must find a new group of super powered beings, otherwise there is no hope for the universe, and I may never be able to become ruler in Frieza’s throne unless I kill him soon! Time is running out…I must end his life, before he ends mine for failing him! He will kill me when he finds out that his private army has been destroyed!”

Zarbon knows what he must do, as he changes his destination to the planet Earth. Maybe he can hide out there for a while, whilst he trains hard, and becomes even more powerful than Frieza. He has heard about the powerful warriors that reside there, maybe they can help him…he shall have to wait and see…

***

Back on the Planet Saiya, the regeneration tank gives a little buzz, and the water in there begins to drain. Korosu opens his eyes, and they are full of awareness. He blasts the glass door open, and walks out, breathing in the stale air. He sniffs gallantly for a while, and a look of horror strikes his face. The air is stale for a reason.

“The…the smell of…of…of death is in the air! The barracks! Otaku! I’m coming guys!” Korosu shouted, rather helplessly. He ran straight through the door of the medical room, knocking it off its hinges, as he lunges on, rather clumsily, attempting to get to his fallen comrades in time. He grabs a scouter off one of the village merchants as he sprints down the street. Korosu starts to press the button on it, but it doesn’t even blink. Korosu desperately tries to make it work, but it remains blank. He takes it off, and crushes it in his hands, dropping the devastated remains on the ground.

‘Whoa, I feel way faster now! I feel much stronger too…..how though? I can’t be as strong as I think I am can I? If I am right, then I must be as strong as Otaku is now! But all that happened after my training was a beating….how could that have contributed….no time to think about that,. I must save my friends!’ thought Korosu as he continues to run down the street. He doesn’t realize what he is going to witness in a few short seconds when he enters the barracks. He has no idea of the carnage that awaits him. He is hoping that everything is going to be alright, but he realizes that he is only trying to fool himself. “EVERYONE HAD BETTER BE OK!” Korosu shouted at the top of his voice, as he skidded to a halt outside the barracks, of which the walls have been cracked, and blown off in places, and the roof is now non-existent. He takes a deep breath, about to step inside to view what has happened.


Chapter 4 - The Price Of Failure

Korosu bursts into the barracks to see a horrifying sight. Saiyan blood plasters the walls, and a few strings of intestines are scattered around too. A tear gathers in the eye of this merciless warrior. He has killed thousands, destroyed countless planets, and never once thought about how they must have felt. The tear starts to stream slowly down his cheek, eventually dropping to the ground, making some of the dry blood run a little. He looks around, desperately trying to find signs of life.

“K…Korosu..” a voice trembled. Coughing is followed afterwards, along with a scream of pain. “Korosu …. over….. here!” the voice said again, this time with a distinct struggle to get the words out.

“Otaku? Is that you?” Korosu asked.
