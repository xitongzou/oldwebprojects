Dragonball Z Final Battle

Battle of Freeza

Fighters:
Good: Son Goku, Son Gohan, Krillin, Piccolo, and Vegeta
Bad: Freeza

Act 1: The Fight of the Century is Between Freeza and Son Goku
As everyone is fighting against Freeza, Goku is getting healed in a rejuvenation tank to make himself ready for the fight. After it seems all it lost, Goku finally comes over and stops Freeza. There is a small fight before the big bang. Goku semi-tests his new strength against Freeza and seems to be doing pretty well. Freeza, however, is not amused. Vegeta is just about to do something that is totally out of character.

Act 2: Vegeta Dies from Freeza
While Freeza was done firing ki blasts at Goku, Vegeta couldn't help but laugh that Freeza will die by Goku. Freeza is done listening to Vegeta and fires a ki blast through his heart. Before Vegeta died, he told Goku all about how is life was tormented by having to follow Freeza's order, all the way to his death. He told him Freeza didn't care for anyone but himself, and, hates everyone he knows. He especially hated me (says Vegeta) and that is why I want you to stop him. With that, Vegeta lies down to rest himself, to his death.

Act 3: Goku's Anger is rising at Kaiouken x20!
Goku tells Vegeta he promises that he will get revenge for Freeza doing all those bad things to him, and, the great battle insures. Goku starts to attack Freeza, and, an exchange of punches, kicks, and ki blasts at each other. After powering up to Kaiouken x20, Goku is just about a match for Freeza, but Freeza always seems to be even better than him. Goku, coming up with a plan, decides to move on and try to kill him with a special attack.

Act 4: Is This the End!? Son Goku Throws the Genki Dama!
After a long wait, the Genki Dama is finally finished, even after Freeza would continually hit and kick Goku, he still held up the Genki Dama. When every last bit of ki energy was necessary, Goku finally threw the Genki Dama at Freeza, while Freeza was just about to destroy the planet. Freeza had no choice but to take the blast head on. Afterwards, everyone was rejoicing.

Act 5: Freeza Takes His Anger on Krillin
After what seemed like Freeza had died, he really hadn't. Just when everyone was making plans to go home, Freeza showed himself again, and was mad as a hornet. He tried to kill Goku first, but Piccolo stepped in and took the blast for him. Piccolo then fell unconscious. Freeza then used a ki attack to bring Krillin in the air, and, make him explode. Goku couldn't believe his eyes that his best friend had died before him. Freeza then told Goku that his little brat was next. Goku couldn't take it anymore, and, went over the top. He then turned into a super Saiyan.

Act 6: That was a Mistake. A Super Saiyan is Born
Son Goku is raging mad. But, how would you feel if your archenemy killed your best friend? Anyways, Goku starts to grow in power, every second. Freeza cannot believe this. What he had feared worse was standing before him. However, Freeza knew he could take him. Goku decided to prove him wrong. After about a couple hard punches to the face, Freeza couldn't take it very much anymore. He wanted to make an offer now Goku couldn't refuse.

Act 7: The Power of Two Awesome and Great Fighters Collide!
Freeza told Goku that he could power up to his 100% form and give him a real challenge since he had transformed. Goku, being a Saiyan, couldn't resist. Kaio Sama disagreed, but Goku knew he could defeat him no matter what. After a death Kamehameha, Freeza didn't want to risk it anymore. He had now decided that enough was enough. He was going to kill Goku now, even though they two were on the planet, he knew a way to kill him. Act 8: Goku Finally Kills the Evil Emperor Freeza shot a Kienzan at Goku that had a tracker on it, to only focus on Goku. Even if it hit a boulder, it would still keep going. Goku had to fly and fly around, until, the Kienzan lost him, and, Freeza decided to start another. Now, two were chasing him, but Goku lost them both as well. Goku kicked the snot out of Freeza for just a bit more, until, one of the Kienzan cut Freeza in half. Freeza had lost his lower torso, half of his tail, and half of his left arm. In the end, Goku gave Freeza some of his power, so he could live, but, Freeza used that power to go against Goku, but, Goku was still more powerful, and, shot Freeza into nothing.
