Submitted for your approval: DBZ stories created by the fans! Enjoy these masterpeices and submit your own to Me if you have them!

For those of you submitting work, here are some guidelines:

  • Send only plain text documents, do not use MS Word!
  • Put a blank line between paragraphs, this helps soooooo much!
  • Do not put your story in the body of an email, send it as an attachment.
  • Tell me how you want to be identified, name/alias and e-mail address.
