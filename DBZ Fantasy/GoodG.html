Android 16


  1. Android #16 is one of Dr.Gero's creations. He was created to be more superior to Android 18 and 17 but because Dr.Gero died before finishing his programing he is lacking in brain power and chooses only to fight with Goku. He is more peaceful than Android 17 and 18 and unlike them he is fully robotic instead of half human half robot. He is forced to fight against Cell to prevent him from absorbing Android 18 and is later destroyed by Cell who crushes his head, causing Gohan to transform into his SSJ2 level.

    Android 18 is the twin sister of Android #17, and was created by Dr.Gero but because of malfunctioning problems had to be shut down. Unlike her brother #18 is not as patient and acts more viciously in her battles. While in the Cell Saga she develops a crush on Krillin and ends up marrying him after Cell is defeated. After that #18 plays a minor role in the Buu Saga fighting in the World Tournament.
