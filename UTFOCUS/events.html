Throughout the year, UT FOCUS is host to a number of VOLUNTEERING and FUNDRAISING programs designed to benefit pre-selected non-profit organizations in Toronto and the GTA. The events anticipated for the upcoming year will consist of both single-day events and events that run the span of the entire academic year.

Both the volunteering and fundraising events have been planned to incorporate a multitude of charitable organizations, ranging from volunteer work that will help assist children in low-income neighbourhoods, to feeding the homeless and working with the elderly.

Of equal importance, UT FOCUS acknowledges the generous support from its members and sponsors, and in recognition of the contributions, hosts a variety of SOCIALS that are meant to treat our supporters to some fun and entertainment.

*Please note - that all proceeds accumulated at any event, will be entirely donated to a charitable beneficiary.
