> About AF

The University of Toronto Federation of Charitable University Students (UTF or UT FOCUS) is a non-profit, student based charity organization. UTF maintains three primary objectives:
  1. To create and promote awareness of individuals who are less fortunate than ourselves
  2. Encourage students to proactively give back to their communities, through participation in social, volunteering and fundraising events
  3. To promote a healthy campus life, through a balance of social and charitable events
UTF has maintained proud partnerships with many prominent non-profit organizations such as the Toronto Raptors Foundation, Toronto Kiwanis Boys and Girls Clubs, and the Scott mission, to name but a few. Our organization is headed by a strong executive committee, each dedicated to improving the lives of those encumbered by adversity.
  • UTF exists as part of a larger organization, recognized as Asian FOCUS (AF), and is but one chapter of the six universities that are distinguished by AF. (For more information, please visit www.asianfocus.org)

  • Although UTF shares the same goals and initiatives that the entire AF organization endorses, our executive committee is determined to create a more unified and inclusive federation at UT this year - our distinction as UTF rather than AF comes from the hope to promote a more diversified ethnic membership base, such that our group does not selectively include or exclude any persons over another.
