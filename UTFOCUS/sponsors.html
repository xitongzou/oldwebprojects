John's International School of Tae Kwon Do
(3880 Midland Ave., Unit 12-15) - 416-297-4381

The school is an internationally recognized Taekwon-Do training centre where students are taught by a certified international instructor in a 5,000 square feet indoor air-conditioned facility. With school rules and a well-developed training system, students will discover their hidden potential and will be able to develop it to the fullest extent. With 30 years of teaching experience, Master Lau fully understands the importance of an instructor's role in the development of a student's self-displine and self-cultivation. A clean, safe and comfortable environment is also an imprtant component to this growth Many parents have come to improve discipline, self-confidence as well as physique, and the truth is that we can filfill and satisfy these wishes.
