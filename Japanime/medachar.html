Ikki Tenryou



Ikki is an intelligent, outgoing boy who loves Medabots. Unfortunately, he was unable to get one until he was able to save enough money. One day he found a very rare medal in the river and this changed everything. Ikki and Metabee don't always agree, but they are there for each other when needed.

Erika



Erika is a very strong-minded girl who is always in search of a good story for her school newspaper. She is also a good childhood friend of Ikki's even though she can be quite bossy. Erika's medabot Brass is always looking out for her.

Henry



Henry is the teenager who works in a convenience store by day. However, his ultra ego is the Phantom Renegade who, by night, collects rare medabot medals. He knows all there is to know about medabots, because 10 years ago, he was the star of the World Robattle Tournament with his Hercules Beetle-type Medabot. He has another ultra ego...a.k.a. Space Medabotter X (or phantom renegade).

Koji



Koji is your spoiled little rich boy. He considers Karin his girlfriend and gets very jealous when others (especially Ikki) pay attention to her. His medabot Samillidon is very tough, but Koji has enough honor not to take advantage of anyone

Karin



Sweet and innocent Karin seems to be the object of many boys affection. She is generous and kind to everyone. She is also the heir to a fortune and attends a private school. Koji thinks of her as his girlfriend and is very protective. Because Karin's medabot has the special healing power, it has never lost a robattle and is considered a legend.

Samantha



Samantha is your basic tomboy. Because she is so tough, she is considered the leader of the screws (a group that includes her,spyke and sloan). She bullies Ikki frequently and has a very feisty electric-type medabot named Peppercat.
