TYu Yu Hakusho is the story of a 14-year-old delinquent by the name of Urameshi Yusuke, who gets into an accident that changes his life forever. With Yusuke's second chance at life Koenma, the son of Enma who is the ruler of the Spirit world, appoints Yusuke as the Spirit Detective for the Spirit world. Yusuke is given new powers and abilities to assist him on his various missions and later gains powerful allies to help him.
