Monster Rancher Review (reviewed by animeNfo.com)



Wow it's amazing how spoiled people are for anime. But let's put aside my 'personal' opinions and get on with the story. It's like Pokemon and Digimon in a way, but lucky enough they don't evolve into bigger powerful monsters(I think, it's been a while since I last saw it). The plot is kind of new, but has that old concept feel to it; boy in game, saves girl, get's monster(usually the most powerful), bad guy summoning something more evil then evil itself. It's old and used a lot, but kids seem to like it, like how we like action and ecchi. I like it a lot, of course I like Digimon(mainly the 3rd season, the other two were so-so) and Pokemon(the beginning that is) too.

Anime Rating:

5.6/10
