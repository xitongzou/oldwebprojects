The Legend of the dragonballs was that if you collected all 7 of the dragonballs, a gigantic dragon will appear and grant you 1 wish. After that wish, all 7 dragonballs will scatter throughout the world once again. The dragon does not grant powerful wishes, though it does grant immortality, which leads the various enemies of the universe to seek these dragonballs. Dragon Ball Z is a sequel to Dragonball following the exploits of a young hero named Goku (Saiyan named Kakarotto). As a baby, Goku was sent to Earth by his father, Bardock, who foresaw a deadly attack on their home planet. Originally deployed on a routine military operation to destroy the planet Earth, along with all its inhabitants, Goku befriends some strange, yet nice people, and changes his agenda from destroying the Earth to protecting it from evil.

2nd saga of the Dragon Ball trilogy follows the story of our hero's adventures through his adult years. Through the Z saga, Goku, along with some old friends, and even some new ones, must protect the universe from any evil encounters.
