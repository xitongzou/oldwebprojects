Hitomi is a highschool girl who plays with tarot cards and is on the track team. One day while she was running to beat time in order to win her first kiss from Amano (her crush), a bright light came from above and a dragon and a young man entered her world through it . She was with her best friend Yukari and Aman when this occurred, but it was only she that could understand what the Young man was saying. The young man defeated the dragon and was once again swallowed by the light, however, Hitomi joined him for the ride. Having landed in Gaia, Hitomi learns that the arrogant young man before her is Van Fanel, heir to the Throne of Fanelia. In order to take the crown, Van had to fight a dragon for an energist which would activate the legendary guimelef, Escaflowne. During his coronation however, Zaibach attacked Fanel and destroyed the city that Van was ruler of for only a few moments. Van and Hitomi escaped using Escaflowne and Van is hellbent, to say the least, to avenge his fallen country. The two endup on unknown territory by the name of Asturia where they meet Allan Schezar, a noble knight of that country, pilot of the guimalef Scherezade and lady's man, rolled into one; from him and the crew of the Crusade Van and Hitomi find allies on their new journey. The story revolves around Hitomi finding herself and sorting her feelings as well as with Van, who has more to learn about himself and who he really is.
