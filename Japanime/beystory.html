Welcome to the world of Beyblade! If you're looking to get into the Beyblade anime series, this will be the best series for you to start your learning. Beyblade first premeired on January 8, 2001 and ended on December 24, 2001 with 51 episodes. Nelvana realized how sucessful the series was and brought it over here in North America. All 51 episodes have already aired, but don't worry--reruns still show on TV, and the series will be on DVD too! Beyblade is the story of a teenager named Tyson Granger who wishes to be the best at the most popular sport in the world--Beyblade! Beyblades are spinning tops. You can add different parts to them, making your Beyblade stronger. In the anime, bit beasts (spirits of animals) can live inside the Beyblade, and be summoned at will to do special attacks. Tyson makes friends with a Beyblade expert Kenny, and a friendly boy who's dad owns a Beyblade shop, Max Tate. The three enter a tournament held in their town, where they meet the mysterious Ray and the champion, Kai Hiwatari. After this tournament, the five boys team up to make a new team, the BBA Team. Traveling around the world, the team hopes to become the ultimate champions of the world. Throughout their adventure, they go through many hardships…can they work together to reach the finals? If you want great character development, action packed battles between monsters and machines, and a great adventure, look no further than Beyblade!
